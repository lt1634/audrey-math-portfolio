Stage 2: Operations

Addition/Subtraction for 4-Year-Olds

1

Understand Addition as Combining

Children learn that addition means putting groups together to make a bigger group.

Example: 3 apples + 2 apples = 5 apples
2

Simple Subtraction

Children understand subtraction as taking away or removing objects from a group.

Example: 5 cookies - 2 cookies = 3 cookies
3

Use Objects for Operations up to 10

Children can solve addition and subtraction problems using physical objects as manipulatives.

Example: Using toy cars, blocks, or fingers to count
4

Word Problems like 'How Many Left?'

Children can solve simple word problems involving addition and subtraction in real-life contexts.

Example: 'I had 7 stickers, gave away 3. How many left?'
5

Basic Equations

Children can recognize and work with simple mathematical equations using symbols.

Example: 2 + 3 = 5, 8 - 2 = 6
