Stage 1: Foundation

Number Recognition and Counting (1-10) for 3-Year-Olds

1

Sing and Say Numbers 1-10

Children can happily recite numbers from 1 to 10, just like a catchy song!

Example: Singing Cantonese number rhymes or counting during MTR rides
2

Count Objects Up to 5

Children can touch each item once and say one number name for each item.

Example: Counting delicious dim sum pieces or colorful building blocks
3

Recognise Numerals 1-5

Children can point to written numbers and say their names when they see them.

Example: Spotting numbers on flashcards, books, or bus tickets
4

Match Quantities to Numerals

Children can connect written numbers to the correct group of objects.

Example: Putting two pineapple buns next to the number '2'
5

Understand 'How Many?'

When asked 'How many?', children can count and tell you the total number.

Example: 'How many red lanterns are hanging?' (child says 'Five!')
